The Empresario Journey

1. The Idea (What)

Empresario is a global business model competition that empowers student innovators to develop their ideas into viable businesses. We welcome concepts from every sector—be it deep tech, social impact, or consumer services.

2. The Blueprint (Why)

We believe every great venture starts with a solid plan. Our goal is to provide the mentorship, resources, and structured feedback necessary to help you craft a business model that is ready for the real world.

3. The Launchpad (Who)

This competition is for the dreamers, the builders, and the disruptors. It's for any student, anywhere in the world, who is passionate about solving problems and is ready to take the first step on their entrepreneurial journey.
